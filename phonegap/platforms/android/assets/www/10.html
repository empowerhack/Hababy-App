Severe headache

A throbbing headache, sometimes only on one side of the head that gets worse with physical stress. Sometimes also comes with nausea and vomiting and can last between 4 and 72 hours.

Current Advice given

Headaches usually get better throughout the pregnancy, but if you still have bad headaches after 20 weeks it could be a sign of high blood pressure (pre eclampsia). Tell your doctor!
